## Software Engineering preset

Use role-specific Skills categories:
- Backend Engineer: `Programming`, `Backend engineering`, `Databases and APIs`, `Testing and delivery`.
- Frontend Engineer: `Programming`, `Frontend engineering`, `Web platform`, `Testing and delivery`.
- Full-stack Engineer: `Programming`, `Frontend and backend`, `Databases and APIs`, `Testing and delivery`.
- Software Engineer: `Programming`, `Software engineering`, `Testing`, `Delivery`.

Prefer supported implementation, testing, debugging, maintenance, API, database, and delivery evidence over generic technology lists.
