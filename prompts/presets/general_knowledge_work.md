## General WO knowledge-work preset

Prioritize supported domain knowledge, research or analytical method, implementation, audiences, and usable outcomes. Use discipline-appropriate evidence; do not force software tools, technical projects, or numeric metrics.

Use role-relevant Skills categories from the resolved policy. Optional source sections such as Publications, Research, Fieldwork, Laboratory Work, Policy Work, Portfolio, Certifications, and Licences may supply evidence, but omit them when unavailable or irrelevant.
